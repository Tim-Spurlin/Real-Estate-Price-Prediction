Model Architecture

Powered by a multi-pillar feature engineering pipeline and stacking ensemble.

🧠 Stacking Regressor

6 base models (Lasso, Ridge, XGBoost, LightGBM, CatBoost, GBR) fused with a Ridge meta-learner.

📐 Feature Engineering

5,000+ synthesized variables via DFS, polynomial interactions, geospatial vectorization, and macroeconomic signals.

🎯 Boruta Selection

Random Forest shadow testing strips noise, keeping only statistically dominant features.

🌍 Geospatial Mapping

Haversine distances to Iowa State University, KMeans neighborhood clustering.

📈 Macroeconomic Data

FRED mortgage rates, Iowa unemployment, and Ames HPI integrated with forward-fill to prevent lookahead bias.

🔒 RMSLE Optimized

Log-transformed target ensures equal penalization of errors across all price tiers.
